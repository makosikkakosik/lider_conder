Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We typically respond to emergency calls within 2-4 hours, depending on your location and current call volume. Same-day service is available for most emergency repairs.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all our services. We'll assess your needs and provide a detailed quote with no obligation to proceed.

What brands of AC systems do you work on?

We service all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, and many others. Our technicians are trained on the latest models and technologies.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include regular inspections, filter changes, and priority scheduling. These plans help prevent costly breakdowns.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. All our technicians are licensed, bonded, and insured. We also carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect your property.

What warranty do you provide on your work?

We provide a 1-year warranty on all repairs and installations. Parts come with manufacturer warranties, and we stand behind all our workmanship.
